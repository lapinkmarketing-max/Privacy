Lapink Data Deletion Request

App: Lapink
Updated: March 23, 2026

You can request deletion of some or all of your Lapink data without deleting your account by using the email link below.

Request data deletion

What to include

  • The email address used for your Lapink account
  • Your mobile number, if applicable
  • Your full name, if available
  • A description of the data you want deleted
  • A note that your account should remain active if you do not want the account deleted

Examples of requests

  • Delete saved profile details that are no longer needed
  • Delete selected notes or non-essential records where legally permitted
  • Delete data that is no longer required while keeping the account active

What may be retained

We may retain limited information where required for legal, security, fraud-prevention, dispute-resolution, service continuity, or accounting purposes.

Processing time

We aim to process verified deletion requests within 7 business days.

If the button above does not open your email app, send your request manually to ferres@gmail.com.
